About Us

Brace yourself, for Cookie Chaos is a bakery like no other. Welcome to the depths of confectionery madness, where anything can (and will!) happen. Led by the quirky owner Jane Doe, our demented design team unleashes its sugar-fueled creative energies daily. They craft wildly imaginative cookies and muffins while pushing the boundaries of culinary insanity. From cookies depicting your favourite political conspiracy theories to muffins oozing a rainbow of questionable flavours, no bake escapes unscathed from our oven of mayhem. So if you're seeking to take your event "over the top" in the deranged way possible, let our loose cannons of crumbling cookies bring the complete party pandemonium. Just know that once you taste our goods, your grip on reality may never be the same!
